Double Chocolate Cookies

These cookies are not for the faint hearted, with their rich, chocolatey flavor, only true chocolate lovers should try to make these. They are not difficult to make, requiring simple household ingredients.

Photo Gallery

The finished product. Cookies are baked to perfection with a nice crisp exterior along with a gooey interior. If made correctly, these cookies are a perfect combination of chocoalatey, salty, and sweet.

You need semi-sweet chocolate chips because they add a slightly stronger choclate flavor to the cookie without an overpowering sweetness.
